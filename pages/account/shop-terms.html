Dieu khoan mo shop tren san

Truoc khi gui ho so mo shop, nguoi dang ky bat buoc doc ky cac dieu khoan ben duoi, dong y voi quy dinh kinh doanh tren san va xac nhan chiu trach nhiem truoc phap luat doi voi hang hoa, giay to va noi dung dang ban.

Quay lai ho so mo shop Ve trang chu

1. Dieu kien gui ho so

  • - Nguoi dung phai co tai khoan hop le tren he thong.
  • - Phai dien day du thong tin ca nhan va thong tin shop du kien.
  • - Phai tai day du giay to theo dung nhom shop dang ky.
  • - Phai tick dong y dieu khoan va xac nhan trach nhiem phap ly truoc khi gui.
  • - Seller khong duoc tu tao shop hoac dang san pham neu chua duoc Admin phe duyet.

2. Ho so shop thuong

  • - Ho va ten nguoi dai dien
  • - Email va so dien thoai
  • - CCCD / CMND hoac giay to dinh danh
  • - Ten shop du kien, dia chi, mo ta, loai hang hoa
  • - Cac tai lieu bo sung neu Admin yeu cau

3. Ho so PetMall / thuong hieu

  • - Ten phap nhan / cong ty
  • - Ma so thue hoac giay dang ky kinh doanh
  • - Giay phep hoat dong neu co
  • - Chung minh quyen so huu thuong hieu / quyen phan phoi
  • - Tai lieu ve nguon goc hang hoa va cam ket chat luong
  • - Dia chi kinh doanh ro rang, nguoi dai dien phap luat

4. Hanh vi bi cam

  • - Ban hang gia, hang nhai, hang kem chat luong
  • - Ban hang khong ro nguon goc, sai mo ta, gay hieu nham
  • - Ban dong vat cam, dong vat hoang da trai phep
  • - Ban thuoc, hoa chat, vat pham vi pham quy dinh phap luat neu khong duoc phep
  • - Gian lan don hang, gian lan danh gia, lua dao, chiem doat tai san
  • - Su dung giay to gia hoac tai lieu khong trung thuc

5. Co che xet duyet va nhan shop

Shop thuong

Danh cho seller thong thuong sau khi duoc phe duyet ho so co ban.

Verified Seller

Danh cho seller co ho so day du, thong tin ro rang va duoc Admin xac minh.

Official Brand

Danh cho brand chinh hang hoac nha phan phoi duoc xac thuc.

PetMall

Danh cho shop quy mo lon, thuong hieu hoac don vi co day du ho so phap ly va dat tieu chuan cao hon.

6. Trach nhiem phap ly va che tai

  • - Nguoi ban tu chiu trach nhiem phap ly voi hang hoa va noi dung do minh dang tai.
  • - San co quyen tu choi, khoa hoac xoa shop neu phat hien vi pham.
  • - Admin phai luu ly do tu choi, tam khoa, cam vinh vien va lich su xu ly vi pham.
  • - Truong hop nghiem trong, shop co the bi xoa vinh vien va thong tin co the duoc chuyen cho co quan co tham quyen.
